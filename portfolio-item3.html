Project Title:      Claire

Your trusted mental health journal

This project was made at Hack the Valley at the University of Toronto!

Today, many youths are struggling with their mental health. Journaling is a great way to collect your thoughts and reflect on the day. While pen and paper is great, with Claire, as you write, you get suggestions from YouTube and Quora about videos and posts based on the things you are writing about.

You can check the code on github!
